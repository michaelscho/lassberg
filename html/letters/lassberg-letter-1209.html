At that time, it was impossible for me to do so immediately, given the joy I felt over your kind consideration for me, I could not find the words. Then came all sorts of unpleasant tasks that stole my joy in everything. I wanted to go to Poland for a longer period, perhaps forever; luckily, nothing came of it.
